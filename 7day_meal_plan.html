💡 Dietitian Tips for Success

🧂 GF Staples to Stock Up On

  • GF tamari (not regular soy sauce)
  • Certified GF white & red miso paste
  • Rice noodles & sweet potato glass noodles
  • GF oyster sauce or coconut aminos
  • GF gochujang (check the label carefully!)
  • GF fish sauce (most are naturally GF)

🥥 Dairy-Free Swaps Used

  • Light coconut milk → replaces cream
  • Coconut yogurt → used in raita & sauces
  • Sesame oil → adds richness & depth
  • Nut butters → snacks and satiety
  • Almond or oat milk → if needed in any baking

🍚 Meal Prep Shortcuts

  • Cook a big pot of rice on Sunday — reheat all week
  • Pre-marinate proteins overnight (chicken teriyaki, tandoori)
  • Make congee in bulk; it keeps 3 days refrigerated
  • Prep chia pudding the night before (Day 4)
  • Chop all veg for the week on Sunday

⚖️ Weight Loss Reminders

Consistency > perfection. If you go over 1200 cal on one day, don't stress — just get back on track. Add a 20–30 min walk daily to support your deficit. Eating slowly and mindfully prevents overeating even on lower-cal plans. Track meals if it helps keep you accountable!

💧 Hydration & Extras

Aim for 8 glasses of water daily. Unsweetened green tea is excellent — it's calorie-free and pairs beautifully with your Asian meal days. Bone broth (GF) is a great low-cal snack option too. Avoid sugary drinks and fruit juices — stick to whole fruit for sweetness.

🔄 Flexibility Notes

Swap proteins freely — chicken ↔ shrimp ↔ tofu ↔ fish. Scale rice portions down by ¼ cup if you want to cut 50–70 more calories. The plan is a guide, not a prison! Enjoy cooking and make each meal yours.
