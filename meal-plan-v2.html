💡 Dietitian Tips & Pantry Guide

🧂 High-Protein Asian Staples

  • Firm & silken tofu (freezer-friendly)
  • Edamame (frozen, ready in 5 min)
  • GF tamari & GF miso paste
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms (flavour + protein)
  • GF fish sauce & GF oyster sauce
  • Canned tuna in olive oil
  • GF collagen powder (odourless, flavourless)

🥦 Low-Carb Swaps Used

  • Cauliflower rice → replaces steamed rice
  • Shirataki noodles → replaces rice noodles in pho
  • Lettuce cups → replaces rice bowls in wraps
  • Zucchini noodles → pasta or noodle substitute
  • Extra eggs → adds protein without carbs
  • Avocado → adds fat + satiety, replaces starch

🍚 Meal Prep Strategy

  • Batch-cook cauliflower rice Sunday — keeps 5 days
  • Hard-boil 6 eggs at the start of the week
  • Pre-marinate chicken (tikka, bulgogi) overnight
  • Make a big batch of bone broth for pho & soups
  • Portion nuts & seeds into snack bags in advance

💪 Why High Protein for Weight Loss?

Protein has the highest thermic effect of food — your body burns ~25–30% of protein calories just digesting it. It preserves lean muscle during a calorie deficit, dramatically reduces hunger hormones (ghrelin), and keeps you full for longer than carbs or fat. Hitting 110g+ daily is a game-changer for sustainable fat loss.

🚫 Yogurt-Free Cooling Options

  • Cucumber-mint-lime juice → cooling dip / dressing
  • Tahini + lemon → creamy sauce without dairy/yogurt
  • Avocado mash → replaces creamy sauces
  • Light coconut milk → for curries & marinades only
  • Silken tofu blended → creamy dip or dressing base

🔄 Flexibility & Adjustments

Swap any protein freely — chicken ↔ tofu ↔ fish ↔ prawns. If you're more active on a day, add ½ cup of brown rice to any dinner. If you need more fat, add an extra ½ avocado or drizzle of tahini. Don't stress over perfect numbers — hitting the protein target matters most!
